How to Use:

Arctic Sea Ice Visualization:

  • Press 'START' to display a map of arctic sea ice extent in the given time period. Arctic sea ice extent is measured in thousands of square miles.
  • Click a year to see the arctic sea ice extent map for that selected year.

Greenhouse Gas Emissions:

  1. Press 'PLAY' to begin animation of greenhouse gas emissions growth over time.
  2. Press 'PAUSE' to stop animation at any given year.
  3. Press 'PLAY' to resume the animation.

  4. Each circle represents 1,000,000 tonnes of greenhouse gas emissions.
